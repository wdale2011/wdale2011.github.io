Biography

Hello there! My name is William Dale and I am a full stack developer specializing in web development living in the greater Los Angeles area. I am a semi-fluent Spanish speaker, an avid gamer, a trumpet player, a novice painter, a voracious reader, and a lifelong learner. I was raised in Herndon, Virginia and graduated with a bachelors degree in Political Science, Spanish, and Economics from Tulane University in New Orleans Louisiana.

After graduating from university, I spent some time in the workforce in both sales and management before deciding that I needed more intellectually stimulating work. In my free time, I began teaching myself to code on CodeAcademy and found programming to be both immensely challenging and rewarding. Following my passion, I moved out West here to LA to join a coding bootcamp and subsequently worked with a team of fellow developers to build my first full web application called "Grolo" for a startup entrepeneur. Some demonstrations of my contributions to that project can be found here in my portfolio.

Since then, I have continued to pursue personal projects as well as more opportunities to expand my technological and professional skillset. If you have any job opportunities that you feel I would be a good candidate for you can reach out to me using the contact info provided. I am also going to start a blog soon that will be linked to this site when completed, so stay tuned!

Thanks for stopping by!

-William M. Dale
